# Free Finance Calculators

A curated collection of the best free online finance calculators for personal finance, investments, loans, and tax planning.

## Online Calculator Tools

### Loan & EMI Calculators
- [EMI Calculator](https://calcota.com) - Calculate your monthly loan EMI instantly
- [Loan EMI Calculator](https://captaincalc.com) - Compare home, car, and personal loan EMIs
- [Mortgage Calculator](https://123calculators.com) - Calculate mortgage payments and amortization
- [Loan Comparison Tool](https://convertmyloan.com) - Compare different loan offers side by side

### Investment Calculators
- [SIP Calculator](https://calcota.com) - Plan your mutual fund SIP investments
- [FD Calculator](https://captaincalc.com) - Fixed deposit maturity and interest calculator
- [PPF Calculator](https://calcuword.com) - Public Provident Fund returns calculator

### Tax & GST Calculators
- [Income Tax Calculator](https://calcota.com) - Estimate your income tax liability
- [GST Calculator](https://captaincalc.com) - Calculate GST amounts for goods and services
- [Salary Calculator](https://calcuword.com) - Net salary after tax deductions
